Rizz
Charisma or the ability to attract a romantic partner. Short for 'Charisma'.
Sigma
A popular, successful, but highly independent person. A "lone wolf."
Aura
The energy or vibe someone radiates. You can 'gain' or 'lose' aura based on your actions.
Cooked
When someone is in a very bad situation or has no chance of winning. "Bro is cooked."
Mewing
A technique of tongue placement to improve jawline. Often used with a 'shhh' gesture.
Delulu
Short for delusional. Usually refers to someone having unrealistic romantic expectations.
